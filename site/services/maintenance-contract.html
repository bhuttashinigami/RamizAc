Maintenance Contract

Home > Services > Maintenance Contract

Do you want your home and workplace comfortable all year round?

Ramiz Hafeez provides unmatched AC Annual Maintenance Contract Services. Regular, scheduled service — known as Planned Preventive Maintenance — must be carried out to keep air conditioning equipment in top working order, optimizing accuracy and efficiency.

Preventive maintenance reduces any possibility of equipment failures before they actually occur and enhances the reliability of equipment by replacing any worn components prior to them failing. The undertaking includes regular and routine cleaning, calibrating & adjusting, lubricating, testing, and where necessary, replacing components to avoid a breakdown.

The capability and thorough training of our staff ensure your unit's equipment is operated with optimum efficiency and its life expectancy is stretched to its maximum potential.

We recommend annual maintenance contracts for all domestic or commercial usage of AC systems to minimize breakdowns and increase energy efficiency.

Benefits of Our Maintenance Contract

  • Optimized system efficiency and performance
  • Reduced risk of unexpected breakdowns
  • Lower energy consumption and bills
  • Extended equipment lifespan
  • Priority emergency response
  • Regular inspections and routine maintenance
  • Warranty coverage on repairs
